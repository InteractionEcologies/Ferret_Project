Do you want to try Tutorial?

Instruction

Setup 1 - Enable Ferret Logging Service

  1. Click 'Tap to Play' button.
  2. Click 'Ferret Logging Service' menu.
  3. Click the toggle switch on the top right corner to turn 'On'.
  4. Click 'Ok'.
  5. Make sure the 'Ferret Logging Service' is now 'On'. Then click the back arrow on the top left corner to return to the Accessibility menu.

Setup 2 - Fill out your ID

  1. Click the triangle button on the bottom left of the phone screen.
  2. Enter your Amazon Mechanical Turk Worker ID (14 characters) and Task ID (specified in the HIT) (***Make sure you enter the correct answers to get paid) After putting IDs, click 'Go to the Application' button.
  3. Once you are on the home screen, you can start the task. You can find applications by clicking the home button(circle button with dots).

Task 1 - Simple Calculation

  1. Launch Calculator app.
  2. Calculate 2*5-3
  3. Hit 'CLR' button.
  4. Go back to the home screen.

Task 2 - Complex Calculation

  1. Launch Calculator app.
  2. Calculate ((3+5)*2-4)/3
    You can either use parentheses to calculate or keep calculating each part.
  3. Hit 'CLR' button.
  4. Go back to the home screen.

Task 3 - Error Handling

  1. Launch Calculator app.
  2. Calculate 10/0
  3. After you see the error, Calculate 10/2
  4. Clear the screen.
  5. Go back to the home screen.

Finished

You have completed all the tasks. You can now close the window. Thank you.
